Model

logit(p) = ln(p/(1-p)) = β₀ + β₁x p = 1 / (1 + e^{-(β₀ + β₁x)})

Exponentiating β gives an odds ratio. If β₁=0.7, OR=exp(0.7)=2.01 (odds roughly doubled per unit x).
