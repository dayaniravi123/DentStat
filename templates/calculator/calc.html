Statistical Formula Reference
Mean
x̄ = Σxᵢ / n
Sum of all values divided by the count of values.
Variance (Sample)
s² = Σ(xᵢ - x̄)² / (n-1)
Average squared deviation from the mean using Bessel's correction.
Std Deviation
s = √s²
Square root of variance; measures spread in original units.
Z-Score
z = (x - μ) / σ
Number of standard deviations a point is from the mean.
IQR
IQR = Q3 - Q1
Interquartile range; robust measure of statistical dispersion.
Coeff. of Variation
CV = (s / x̄) × 100
Relative variability expressed as a percentage of the mean.
Binomial P(X=k)
C(n,k) · pᵏ · (1-p)ⁿ⁻ᵏ
Probability of exactly k successes in n independent Bernoulli trials.
Poisson P(X=k)
(λᵏ · e⁻λ) / k!
Probability of k events in a fixed interval given mean rate λ.
